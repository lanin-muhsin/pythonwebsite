About Us

The University of Sulaymaniyah, located in the vibrant city of Sulaymaniyah, Iraq, is a renowned institution dedicated to academic excellence and community advancement. Established in 1968, the university has played a pivotal role in shaping the intellectual landscape of the region. With a commitment to providing quality education and fostering research and innovation, the University of Sulaymaniyah serves as a hub for learning, collaboration, and cultural enrichment. Through its diverse academic programs, state-of-the-art facilities, and engagement with the local community, the university continues to empower students and contribute to the social and economic development of Kurdistan and beyond.

Departments

The University of Sulaymaniyah offers a wide range of departments and academic programs to suit diverse interests and career goals. Some of the departments include:

  • Department of Computer Science
  • Department of Engineering
  • Department of Medicine
  • Department of Law
  • Department of Business Administration
  • Department of Social Sciences
  • Department of Humanities
  • Department of Natural Sciences
  • Department of Education

Each department offers undergraduate, graduate, and professional programs designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills needed for success in their chosen fields. Explore our departments to find the program that aligns with your interests and aspirations.
